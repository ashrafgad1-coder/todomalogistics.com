About TODOMA

TODOMA was founded to solve a problem most delivery businesses face but rarely name directly: operations that depend on people are inherently fragile unless someone is actively managing them.

We act as an operational partner — not a rider supplier — providing fleet management, managed delivery operations, and broader logistics support services so our clients can focus on growing their business rather than running it.

Greater Cairo is one of the densest, fastest-moving delivery markets in the region — high order volumes, congested routes, and rapidly scaling demand from quick commerce, e-commerce, distribution, and logistics operators alike. Operating reliably here requires more than coverage; it requires structured, hands-on management of fleets day to day.

TODOMA is built specifically for this market: a Phase 1 focus on Greater Cairo, with the operational discipline to prove reliability before expanding further across Egypt.
